Frequently asked questions

Is StudyMetrics free to use?

Yes — both tools are completely free, with no account, no usage limits, and no watermark of any kind. Everything runs in your browser.

Is my data uploaded anywhere?

No. Every calculation — unit conversion or GPA math — happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing you enter is transmitted to a server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does the GPA calculator match my school's exact scale?

It uses the standard US 4.0 scale (A=4.0 down to F=0.0, with +/- increments), which is the most common convention — but grading scales vary by institution, and some schools weight honors or AP courses differently. Check your school's official policy if you need an exact, binding GPA.
