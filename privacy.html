Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

StudyMetrics ("we," "us") operates studymetrics.app. This page explains what data is and isn't collected when you use the unit converter and GPA calculator.

Your data is never uploaded

Both tools run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Any values, course names, grades, or credit hours you enter are processed locally on your device. Nothing is sent to, stored on, or logged by our servers.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display advertising served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Analytics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics tools to understand aggregate site traffic, such as page views and general geographic region. This data is anonymized and is not linked to anything you enter into either tool.

Third-party links

This site may link to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

Not an official academic record

The GPA calculator is an estimation tool based on the standard US 4.0 scale and the numbers you provide. It is not connected to any school's official records and should not be treated as a binding or authoritative GPA — check with your registrar for anything official.

Children's privacy

StudyMetrics does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
